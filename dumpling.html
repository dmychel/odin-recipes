Pork Soup Dumpling

Home
Soup dumplings cooked to perfection

Ingredients

  • ½ lb pork skin
  • 1 lb (450g) pork neck bones
  • Water
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Salt
  • Soy Sauce
  • Flower
  • Gelatin
  • whole onion
  1. Prepare the chicken stock jelly cubes
  2. Put pork into food processor for 30-60 seconds
  3. Add pork and spices into a mixing bowl and mix together well
  4. Prepare lightly dusted clean work surface
  5. Roll each piece into a round disc about 3 – 3 ¼ inches diameter
  6. Prepare bamboo steamer
  7. Place 1 tabelspoon of filling in each bun
  8. Place the buns in the lined steamer basket, about 1 1/2 – 2 inches apart
  9. Steam in a metal steamer pot, let it steam over high heat for 8 minutes
