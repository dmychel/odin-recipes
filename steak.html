Amazing Sirloin Steak

Ingredients

  • 1 beef sirloin steak, at room temperature
  • Salt to taste
  • 3 tablespoons high-heat cooking oil, or as needed
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
    1. Preheat cast iron skillet over medium-high heat
    2. Drizzle oil onto hot skillet. Add steak and cook for 30 seconds. Flip steak, cook for 30 seconds, then flip again. Continue flipping and cooking 30 seconds on each side
    3. Remove steak from the pan when it has developed a brown crust, is beginning to firm, and is hot and slightly pink in the center, about 3 to 4 minutes of total cooking time
